BFE - Before First Empire
FE - First Empire
NE - New Empire

 - 200 million years ago
	 - The Plasticene Age.
		 - Nothing is known of this society aside from the layer of microplastics found in the geological samples from across the planet.
		 - The people of this era are estimated to have mined out 99.9% of the oil and coal resources on Zandralor
 - 50 million years ago 
	 - The Impact 
		 - Asteroid collides with Zandralor, infusing the planet with magic, and splitting the continents apart
 - 30,000 years ago
	 - Estimated beginning of the Dark Age.
 - 25,000 years ago
	 - Fourth age reaches its peak, a medieval civilization with magic. Very little is known about this era
 - 20,000 years ago
	 - The Arch-Lich Vecna rises to power and extinguishes all life on the planet. He ascends to godhood, then dies as his followers slowly fade away.
 - 10,000 years ago
	 - The Modern Age begins as agriculture is rediscovered
	 - Oldest evidence of civilization found on Xulai is from this period
 - 9,000 years ago (4,000 BFE)
	 - Oldest pyramids in Eisleron are built
 - 5,128 years ago - 1 FE
	 - First Emperor of Yù dìguó (Jade empire) unites the many kingdoms of northeast Xulai for the first time
 - 3485 FE
	- First empire collapses, leading to the Warlord Era 
 - 3749 FE / 1 NE
	 - End of the Warlord Era
	 - Founding of the New Empire by Yan Sung-Li
